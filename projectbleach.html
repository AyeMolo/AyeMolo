Information

Project B is an in-development Bleach-themed game
on the popular online gaming platform, Roblox.
Inspired by the hit anime and manga series, Project B
aims to bring the world of Bleach to life in a unique
and exciting way for players of all ages.

In Project B, players will be able to create their
own custom characters, each with their own unique
abilities, and explore a vast world filled with dangerous
enemies and exciting challenges. With a variety of
weapons and techniques at their disposal, players
will need to master their skills and work together
with other players to defeat powerful bosses and unlock
new areas.

One of the key features of Project B is its focus on
PvP (player vs player) combat. Players will be able
to engage in intense battles with other players from
around the world, testing their skills and strategies
against some of the toughest opponents around.

In addition to the PvP gameplay, Project B will also
feature a rich story mode, complete with cutscenes,
dialogue, and missions that will allow players to
experience the world of Bleach in a more immersive way.
Whether players prefer to explore the world on their
own or team up with friends to take on tough challenges,
there will be plenty of content to keep them engaged for hours on end.
